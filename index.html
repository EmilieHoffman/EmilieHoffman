Not Your Average “Design Shop”

Based in Crystal Lake, Illinois, EMILIE HOFFMAN DESIGN STUDIO LLC partners with healthcare, fintech, manufacturing, and civic teams that operate under real-world constraints. We turn requirements into creative direction and compliance into compelling experiences. Our work spans brand systems, digital product UI, environmental/wayfinding graphics, and packaging & labeling audits. We are used to redlines from legal, feedback from clinicians, and stakeholder boards that need clear rationales. Every deliverable ships with documentation, spec sheets, and developer-ready tokens so execution is as smooth as the concept.

Specialized Services That Move The Needle

Regulated Packaging & Labeling

We design and audit packaging for medical devices, supplements, and industrial products, aligning structure, copy, and hierarchy with CFR/FDA and EU MDR expectations. You get master artwork, dielines, and language matrices that scale. We also create launch-ready imagery for retail and e-commerce.

Accessible Product UI & Design Systems

From dashboards to data-entry workflows, we craft interfaces that balance speed with safety. Our systems include WCAG 2.2 contrast tokens, keyboard paths, and error-state patterns, plus Figma libraries and developer docs. We regularly collaborate with QA to validate real-world edge cases.

Wayfinding & Environmental Graphics

Hospitals, campuses, and civic spaces benefit from clear, anxiety-reducing navigation. We map decision points, plan sign families, and prototype sight-lines on location. Fabrication-ready drawing sets include materials, mounting details, ADA type specs, and phasing plans for contractors.

How We Work — A Three-Step Path

  1. Scope & Evidence

    We clarify goals, constraints, and regulators, then gather artifacts—SOPs, audit notes, device photos, analytics. You receive a short plan with success metrics and an exact timeline.

  2. Design Sprints with Checkpoints

    Weekly drops include options, rationales, and live prototypes. Stakeholders weigh trade-offs early while we run quick tests for accessibility, readability, and task success.

  3. Handover & Support

    Finals ship as production-ready files: print-spec artwork, Figma libraries, token JSON, and sign schedules. We support vendors and developers until your solution is live.
